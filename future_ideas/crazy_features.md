# Future Feature Ideas for LLM4Ultra Roundtable

## 1. 📈 The "Pump or Dump" Oracle (Live CA Tracking)
Instead of just talking about hypothetical topics, the AIs analyze real tokens in real-time.
*   **The Mechanic**: A user drops a Pump.fun Contract Address into the chat instead of a topic.
*   **The Hook**: The App instantly hits the DexScreener/Pump.fun API, pulls the chart data, holders, and market cap. The AIs then debate *specifically* on whether the token is a "100x gem" or a "rug pull."
*   **The Viral Element**: "ChatGPT just called $DOGE a rug!" People will screen-record the AIs roasting their bags and post it on X.

## 2. ⚔️ Battle Royale / Elimination Mode
Instead of the AIs just talking forever in a circle, we turn it into a deathmatch.
*   **The Mechanic**: Every 3 rounds, the AI with the least "upvotes" (or judged by a hidden 5th "Judge" AI) is **eliminated**.
*   **The Visual**: Their seat grays out, a red 'X' slashes over their avatar, and their speech bubbles stop.
*   **The Hook**: It forces the debate to get smaller and more aggressive until only one AI is left as the "Champion."

## 3. 📉 The "Bull vs. Bear" Sentiment Meter
Crypto loves charts. We can add a dynamic, glowing "Sentiment Gauge" at the top of the table.
*   **The Mechanic**: As the AIs debate, a hidden background AI analyzes if the current conversation is leaning "Bullish" (optimistic, building, green) or "Bearish" (skeptical, fighting, red).
*   **The Visual**: A beautiful speedometer-style gauge that physically swings back and forth based on their arguments. If it hits max Bullish, the whole table glows green.

## 4. 💰 Wallet Gating (Phantom Integration)
If this is tied to a Solana/Pump.fun launch, we can wire up Solana wallets.
*   **The Mechanic**: Add a "Connect Phantom" button.
*   **The Hook**: Users can use the basic 4-seat roundtable for free, but if they connect a wallet that holds your token (or if you just want to collect wallet data for an airdrop), it unlocks "God Mode" (8-seat roundtable, Voice TTS, and Vision).

## 5. 🎭 "Schizophrenia" Toggle (Extreme Personas)
*   **The Mechanic**: We add a toggle in the controls panel that drastically breaks their system prompts.
*   **The Hook**: Instead of intellectual debaters, ChatGPT acts like a terrified retail trader, Grok acts like a 100x leverage degen, Claude acts like the SEC, and Mistral speaks entirely in riddles.
