The Thesis & Lore

We are entering an era of automated surveillance and AI detectors. Academic institutions and corporate grading systems are cracking down, utilizing rudimentary software to flag AI-generated work. LLM4 is the antidote.

When you submit a problem or an assignment to the LLM4 Roundtable, it doesn't just generate a generic response. It runs the query continuously through a customizable roundtable of up to 8 leading AI minds (including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Llama, DeepSeek, Mistral, and Qwen). They debate, refine, and iterate on the solution until the selected models reach an absolute consensus.

Because the final output is a synthesized product of a multi-agent debate rather than a single predictive text string, it creates a "perfected solution" that completely bypasses standard plagiarism and AI detection systems. It gives the user the ultimate, undetectable edge.

Phase 1: V1 Trial Phase (Current)

The current build of the LLM4 AI Roundtable is a Version 1.0. This is an initial proof-of-concept running on a live API.

Phase 2: Account Connectivity & Mobile App

As the ecosystem grows and stabilizes, we will introduce a dedicated Mobile App alongside an email connect system. This will allow users to save their transcripts on-the-go, track their debate histories, and customize their agent prompts without relying on the early beta web framework.

Phase 3: The Permanent Solution

We aren't just building a website; we are building an infrastructure. All developer fees and project revenue are dedicated directly to improving the technology to reach our permanent solution: Full Browser Access and LLM4 Extensions.

This means the power of the multi-agent roundtable will eventually live directly in your browser, allowing you to highlight text anywhere on the web, summon the roundtable, and automatically deploy the perfected solution. Key extension features will include:

  • "Invisible" Ghostwriting Draft notes in any input box (X, Discord, Emails) and hit a hotkey to have the roundtable seamlessly debate and replace it with a perfected synthesis.
  • Real-Time Fact Consensus Toggle "Auto-Debunk" while reading articles or threads to have the council passively scan, cross-reference, and highlight contradictions or biases.
  • Competitor X-Ray Scan complex smart contracts, whitepapers, or landing pages for the roundtable to instantly output a multi-perspective breakdown of strategic flaws and vulnerabilities.
  • Dynamic Council Weights Swap completely different model versions mid-debate and adjust the influence of each seat.
  • Live Audio Output Turn on text-to-speech to have the extension read the debated, finalized output aloud to you in real-time as you work.
